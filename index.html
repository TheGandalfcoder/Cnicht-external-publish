About Us

Our Story

Here at Cnicht External Cleaning Services, we take immense pride in offering you the finest cleaning services in Gwynedd. As a family-run business with over a decade of experience, we deliver top-notch cleaning solutions tailored to your needs.

Our Mission

As a family-owned and eco-conscious cleaning company, our mission is simple: to provide exceptional cleaning services while preserving the environment. We specialise in all aspects of external cleaning for domestic & commercial properties.
